Red Cabbage Salad

Ingrediant

  • 2 ½ cups shredded red cabbage.
  • 1 (10 ounce) can mandarin oranges, drained.
  • 1 green onion, chopped.
  • ¼ cup sweetened dried cranberries.
  • ¼ cup pine nuts.
  • ⅓ cup canola oil.
  • ¼ tablespoon white sugar.
  • 2 piches salt.

Directions

  1. Step 1

    - Lightly toss red cabbage, mandarin orange segments, green onion, and carnberries together in a salad bowl.

  2. Step 2

    - Toast pine nuts in small skillet over medium heat, stirring constantly, until fragrant and lightly browned,

    about 2 minutes.Remove immediately to a bowl and let cool.

  3. Step 3

    - whisk canola oil, vinegar, sugar, and salt in abowl until sugar and salt have dissolved.

  4. Step 4

    - Stir toasted pine nuts into salad and pour dressing over the top. Toss again to coat salad with dressing.
